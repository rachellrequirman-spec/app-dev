# My Favorite Series: Kiss him not me!

"Kiss Him, Not Me!" (Watashi ga Motete Dousunda) is a Japanese anime series. 
The story revolves around Kae Serinuma, a high school student and a dedicated otaku and fujoshi (a fan of Boys' Love). She is overweight and spends most of her time fantasizing about the male characters in her favorite anime and manga getting together.

 **Genre:** Romantic comedy, reverse harem, slice of life.
  **Characters:** Kae Serinuma, Yuusuke Igarashi, Hayato Shinomiya, Asuma Mutsumi
